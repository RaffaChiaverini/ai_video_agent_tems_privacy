Privacy Policy

App Name: video_agent

Developer: The dev fella

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how video_agent handles information when you use the application in connection with TikTok’s OAuth and Content Posting API.

2. Information Collected

The application may temporarily process the following information:

  • TikTok OAuth access tokens
  • Video files selected by the user for upload
  • Basic TikTok account identifiers required by the API

3. Use of Information

Information is used only to:

  • Authenticate users with TikTok
  • Upload or post video content at the user’s request

4. Data Storage

video_agent does not permanently store personal data, access tokens, or video files. Data is processed transiently and discarded after the requested action is completed.

5. Data Sharing

No data is sold or shared with third parties. Data is transmitted only to TikTok as required to perform the requested API actions.

6. User Control

Users may revoke the application’s access at any time through their TikTok account settings.

7. Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect data during transmission and processing.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the application indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
